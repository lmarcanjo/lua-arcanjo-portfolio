Case study

Astra-QA: AI applied to Quality Engineering.

Problem

QA teams often spend too much time creating repetitive artifacts such as test cases, BDD scenarios, test plans and automation drafts.

Solution

Astra-QA uses AI to support QA artifact generation, helping professionals move faster while keeping human review and quality strategy at the center.

Impact

The project demonstrates how AI can improve QA productivity and allow teams to focus more on risk analysis, product quality and delivery confidence.
